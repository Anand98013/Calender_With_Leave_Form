About PM SHRI Kendriya Vidyalaya

Our Vision

PM SHRI Kendriya Vidyalaya is a center of academic excellence committed to nurturing the potential of each child. Our goal is to develop students into confident, responsible citizens, equipped with values, skills, and knowledge that lead them to success in all spheres of life.

Our Mission

We strive to provide a holistic learning environment through quality education, modern teaching methodologies, and inclusive practices. The school embodies discipline, innovation, and community engagement — ensuring every student thrives in a rapidly changing world.

What We Stand For

Excellence

Dedicated to achieving the highest standards in education and performance.

Innovation

Encouraging creativity and forward-thinking approaches in learning.

Integrity

Building a culture rooted in honesty, respect, and transparency.

Inclusivity

Celebrating diversity and ensuring equal opportunities for all students.

PM SHRI Kendriya Vidyalaya Sasaram – Committed to excellence in education and holistic development of students.
